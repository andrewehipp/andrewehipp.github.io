ezExchanges

Identity & Style Guide

ezExchanges is an online financial assistant for restaurants. As a new company that offers an online service they needed a Logo and Style Guide for their website.

The decision was made to design a style guide since pages on the site are generated with different combinations of content for each user. This gave the client flexibility while creating the site but removed the large cost of designing dozens of pages.
